»The Always Evil Game« is an experimental Game that is only controlled by your voice.

The Game was developed during the summer term 2016 at Stuttgart Media University as part of the Game Development Lecture in the Computer Science and Media course by Tobias Schneider and Daniel Grießhaber.

It's a reminiscence of the old Zorg games developed by the MIT in 1980. Furthermore, it is an experiment to see how well games can be played by using nothing but one's voice.
